CAUSES OF HEART DISEASE

    1.Smoking
    2.High blood pressure.
    3.High cholesterol
    4.High lipoprotein
    5.Lack of regular exercise
    6.Diabetes
    7.Thrombosis

SYMPTOMS

    1.Chest pain
    2.Shortness of breath
    3.Pain in the neck
    4.Numbness

TREATMENT

    1.Blood-thinning medicines
    2.Diuretics
    3.Angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitors
